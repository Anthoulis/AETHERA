About AETHERA

Premium honey from Kos Island, crafted with respect for nature and the bee.

Our Story

AETHERA is a family effort rooted in Kos. We harvest in small batches, keep processes simple and clean, and let the island’s flora speak through every jar.

What We Value

  • Purity: Unblended, 100% natural honey.
  • Traceability: Clear origin for each batch.
  • Sustainability: Practices that protect biodiversity.
